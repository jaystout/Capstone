Spinblock

App design, personal project

Role
UI/UX Personal Project
Softwares Used
Invision Studio, Figma
Year
2020
Duration
March 2020 - March 2020

Problem

Spinblock, a Table Tennis club located in Indianapolis, Indiana has a website, but didn't have an app for players and scheduling coaches.

Solution

Designed an app for Spinblock as a personal project (low to medium fidelity) in Invision Studio with interactions to get experience for players to schedule lessons with coaches and login/sign up. I also created a screen in Figma of the website.
