Fyscal Intern Work

I worked for a couple months at Fyscal as a UI/UX intern.

Role
UI/UX Design Intern
Contributions
Wireframing, prototyping, usability testing, presenting
Year
2020
Duration
August 2020 - January 2020

Problem

Fyscal, being a startup financial education company needed their UI designed for their online financial literacy course. They had a direction and vision, but needed to have designs.

Solution

After conducting research, I created low fidelity sketches, user personas, and chose colors I thought suited the brand best. Throughout my process we had weekly meetings and emphasized iterative design. In total, I created a design system, made components, low fidelity and high fidelity wireframes, and made an interactive prototype in Figma.

Sketches

For the first part of my ideation phase, I created sketches of rough ideas for different pages.
