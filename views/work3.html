FoodieX

Screen Design, project

Role
UI/UX Design personal project
Software Used
Figma
Year
2020

Problem

For a design test, XTern gave a prompt to design a food delivery and ordering screen for "FoodieX".

Solution

After conducting research, I created low fidelity sketches, user personas, and chose colors I thought suited the brand best. I made some screens that show what happen when certain actions are taken as well.
